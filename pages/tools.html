Dividend Reinvestment (DRIP) Simulator

When you reinvest dividends instead of taking them as cash, you buy more shares which pay more dividends — creating a powerful compounding cycle. This simulator shows you the difference between taking dividends as cash versus reinvesting them, and calculates your annual dividend income in the final year. Dividend reinvestment is one of the most powerful wealth-building strategies available.

Portfolio Drawdown Analyzer

A "drawdown" is the percentage drop from your portfolio's peak value. This tool reveals a critical asymmetry most investors don't know: a 50% loss requires a 100% gain just to break even — not 50%. Enter your portfolio value, the drawdown percentage, and your expected recovery rate to see exactly how long it would take to get back to where you started. Understanding drawdown is essential for risk management.
